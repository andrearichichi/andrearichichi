Hi, I’m Andrea Richichi, currently pursuing a Master’s in Artificial Intelligence Systems at the University of Trento and working as an AI Research Intern and Master’s Thesis student at IDSIA (USI-SUPSI) in Lugano.
My work focuses on AI for robotics, where I explore how simulation and machine learning can be combined to build realistic robot digital twins. In particular, I work with ROS2 and Unity to create environments where robots and simulations interact in real time.
Previously, at the Bruno Kessler Foundation, I worked on VolleyVisionAI, a computer vision system that tracks ball trajectories and player movements to support tactical analysis in volleyball.

Always curious, always building. If it involves machine learning, robotics, computer vision, or creative problem-solving, count me in.
